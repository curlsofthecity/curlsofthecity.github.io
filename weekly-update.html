This weeks inspiration:

Each week I love to share helpful tips that keep me organized and help me track my progress

  1. Google Calendar: an organized girl dream. Whether you have an Iphone or Android, this cloud bases calendar is super accessable very user friendly. Bonus: It synces with your gmail seamlessly!
  2. Planoly: A visual planners and scheduling tool to help you create a perfect Instagram layout!
  3. Google Drive: While this may seem a little repetative, there is trully nothing better than the convenience of cloud storage; keeping everything at your finger tips is the secret to success.
  4. Trello:Last but not least, Trello. A productivity platform that allows you to keep track of your tasks and work collaboratively without he hassle of 1000 emails updating eachother on edits that you've made.
